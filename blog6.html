Alien Attacks! (Video Game Prototype)

By Juan Moreno

My role was: Programmer

Game Credits

I would like to credit and thank Brackeys for providing the starter template and the shooting at enemy tutorial. And Alexander Zotov for his series of videos of how to add a score, blood effects, game overs etc.

Known Issues, Instructions and Reflection

- The Player does not explode when it touches the enemy. It just brings up the "Game Over" Screen

- Game continues even after a game over.

- Players had some difficulty getting on the structures.

- Issues with Tittle Screen.

- 2nd Enemy clas not working as intended

The Game is played by using the arrow keys. Left and Right to move, down to crouch and up to jump. You shoot with the space bar.

The game overall, still needs some work. But it works as intended, even with its issues. It sure was quite a journey getting stuff to work properly and how I wanted it, and although to some extent it didn’t, it still felt like an accomplishment. The game received more positive feedback after I made the suggested changes (difficulty and fixed animations) and that was quite nice to hear. What I would change from the game would be trying to fix the issue with the alien not exploding when he touches the officer, since the player could still move after the game over and the alien did not explode/disappear. Also players were still having some difficulty killing officers, jumping to the structures… which I found very weird considering the officers don’t shoot. Only 1 person out of around 7 or people I’ve seen, have actually made it to the end. Which calls for modification to the level, so I would maybe make it easier to see the enemies or something. But aside from that I’m glad the game over and restart screen actually works and the officer exploding animation looks as planned. It may not look like much but it truly was difficult and time consuming in some parts. Especially with the errors and trying to make the animations work properly.
